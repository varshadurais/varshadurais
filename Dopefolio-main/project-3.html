Project Overview

DesignSpark Bootcamp was conducted for higher secondary students in Tirunelveli to introduce the principles of Design Thinking through practical learning. The session explained the five stages of Design Thinking—Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test—using relatable real-life examples. Interactive discussions and hands-on activities encouraged students to observe everyday problems, think creatively, and collaborate in teams. The bootcamp aimed to improve communication, teamwork, critical thinking, and innovation while making learning enjoyable and meaningful. The project presentation and supporting materials were developed using Visual Studio Code with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, creating an engaging and responsive learning experience.

Many higher secondary students focus mainly on theoretical knowledge and receive limited opportunities to solve real-world problems creatively. They often hesitate to express ideas, collaborate effectively, or develop innovative solutions. This bootcamp addressed these challenges by introducing a structured Design Thinking approach through interactive activities and relatable examples. The objective was to build confidence, enhance problem-solving abilities, encourage teamwork, and inspire students to develop a human-centered mindset for future academic, personal, and professional success.
